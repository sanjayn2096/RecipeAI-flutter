Common questions

Where can I save recipes from?

You can save recipes from web links, pasted text, and photos of printed or handwritten recipes. Paste a URL or take a photo of a cookbook page.

Can I use Sous Chef for free?

Yes. The free plan gives you recipe ideas, imports, grocery lists, and a 3-day meal planner each day. Premium adds unlimited use, pantry scan, a 7-day planner, and cook help.

Is Sous Chef on Android?

Yes. Get it on Google Play. iOS is coming soon.

Are the recipes always correct?

The app uses AI to suggest recipes. Results can be wrong or incomplete. Check ingredients, amounts, and food safety yourself, especially for allergies. This is not medical or nutrition advice.

Why use Sous Chef?

It puts recipe ideas, saving recipes, grocery lists, meal planning, and step-by-step cooking in one app, based on how you eat and shop.
